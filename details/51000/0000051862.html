ENGL 215: Fiction Down Under (2006-2007, Semester 2)

Students read novels and short stories by 20 to 30 Australian and New Nealand authors, including Keri Hulme, Janet Frame, Frank Sargeson, Patricia Grace, Henry Lawson, David Malouf, Richard Flanagan, and Kim Scott. They encounter distinctive voices and strategies, and they discuss issues of cultural identity, natural environment, indigenous peoples, and gender.
