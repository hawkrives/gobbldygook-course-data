MUSIC 212C: Aural Skills and Improvisation IV (0.25) (2006-2007, Semester 2)

This course continues the aural study of chromatic harmony, melodies and 20th-century rhythms introduced in Music 211. Students also work on basic improvisation skills, including working from jazz chord symbols, improvising melodies from given guidelines, improvising cadenzas and improvising jazz melodies. Prerequisites: Music 211 and 213.
