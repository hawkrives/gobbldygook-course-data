SWRK 373: Culturally Competent Practice (2012-2013, Semester 1)

This course immediately precedes the Field Practicum (Social Work 380). Students integrate principles and skills of culturally competent assessment, planning, intervention, and evaluation with diverse clients - individuals, families, groups, organizations and communities. They roleplay diverse practice situations and articulate implications of social policy for cross-cultural practice Brief immersion opportunities in diverse communities are included. Additional fee. Prerequisite: Social Work 261. For senior majors only. Offered annually in September.
