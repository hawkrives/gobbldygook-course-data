PHYS 374B: Classical Mechanics (2012-2013, Semester 1)

This course is an analytical and computational study of Newtonian mechanics, including the harmonic oscillator, central force motion, non-linear oscillators, chaos, and an introduction to the Lagrangian formulation. Students use computers extensively. Prerequisites: Physics 232 and Mathematics 230. Offered annually in the fall semester.
