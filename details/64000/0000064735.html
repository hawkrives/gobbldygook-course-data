MUSIC 233: Music in China (2008-2009, Interim)

This course explores Chinese music, including art, popular, and sacred styles. Students study these styles in a variety of settings, from the concert hall to coffee houses in Shanghai. Students also examine the role of music in Chinese society, observing how Western-style music has influenced that role, and study how certain Chinese composers have attempted to integrate traditional and Western styles.
