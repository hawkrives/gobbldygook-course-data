PSYCH 220: Socioemotional Development in Cultural Context (2009-2010, Interim)

This course explores children's socioemotional and self development from a cultural and ecological perspective. How do children become emotional beings and cultural selves? Students examine the roles of multiple factors, including parental, extrafamilial, and cultural processes, and discuss how they interact to shape children's sense of self and identity, their emotional experiences and relationships with others. Prerequisite: Psychology 125. Offered most years.
