DANCE 310: Modern III (0.50) (2009-2010, Semester 1)

This course builds on the concepts and components of Modern Dance I and II. Open to majors and other students with instructor's permission. Prerequisite: Dance 210/211 (at least twice) and/or permission of the instructor. Offered each semester.
