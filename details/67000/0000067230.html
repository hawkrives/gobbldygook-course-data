MATH 128A: Honors Calculus II (2009-2010, Semester 1)

This course covers the material in Mathematics 126 in greater depth and includes supplementary material. Prerequisite: 4 or 5 on AP Calculus AB exam or permission of Mathematics Placement Director. Credit may be earned for either Mathematics 126 or 128, but not both. Offered each semester.
