MATH 109: Preparation for Calculus (2009-2010, Semester 1)

This course is designed for students who need additional preparation before taking calculus. The course emphasizes functions, including polynomial, exponential, logarithmic, and trigonometric. Enrollment requires permission of the Director of Mathematics Placement. Offered annually in the Fall Semester.
