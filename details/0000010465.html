DNCTE 310: (0.50), 311 (0.25) Modern III (2005-2006, Semester 1)

This modern section builds on the concepts and components of Modern Dance I and II. This section is open to Companydance members and general students with instructor's permission. Offered both semesters. 311 can be repeated with instructor's permission. Prerequisite: Dance 211.
