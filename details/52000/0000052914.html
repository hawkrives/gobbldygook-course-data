MATH 117: Gateways to Mathematics (2006-2007, Interim)

Students learn principles of mathematical thinking by investigating a particular mathematical topic. Recent topics included dynamic geometry, mathematics of games and cryptology. Students investigate ideas through technical and non-technical reading and problem solving, introducing them to mathematical literature and exposition. Offered both semesters. The course is intended for all students .
