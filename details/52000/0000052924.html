MUSIC 235: Music In Christian Worship (2006-2007, Interim)

Students study the musical history of hymnody and its relationship to the history of the church. In addition, this course explores the Biblical Psalms and their use in worship of the church and includes references to their musical settings in Gregorian chant; Anglican chants, responsorial settings and choral music. Offered during Interim.
