MATH 348: Topology (2006-2007, Semester 1)

This course is an introduction to topological spaces and their structure from point-set, differential and algebraic points-of-view. Topics may include separation axioms, compactness, connectedness, classification of surfaces, homology, fundamental group and others. Prerequisite: Math 244 or 252. Last offered in 2002-03 and alternate years.
