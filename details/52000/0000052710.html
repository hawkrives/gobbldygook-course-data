DANCE 115: Movement for the Performing Artist (0.50) (2006-2007, Semester 1)

This studio course introduces students to the movement concepts and vocabulary of modern dance, ballet, tap and jazz and Laban Movement Analysis and their application to performance in theater, music and dance. Emphasis is on perceiving, describing and experiencing the movement possibilities for the director/actor, conductor/musician, choreographer/dancer. A ticket/transportation fee is required for trips to professional theaters in the Twin Cities.
