MUSIC 222: Counterpoint (2011-2012, Summer Session 1)

To deepen awareness and understanding of performance music, and to gain tools to produce textural interest in composition, students study the contrapuntal techniques of composers from the Renaissance, Baroque, and Modern periods including Palestrina, Bach, and Barber. Prerequisite: Music 214. Offered fall semester 2011-12 and alternate years.
