GERM 250: Speaking German (2005-2006, Semester 2)

Moves students from intermediate to advanced oral proficiency through the use of German TV, films and media sources. Focuses on higher-order functions such as paragraph-length narration, presenting opinions, group discussions and individual presentations, as well as dramatic readings, interviews, debates and conversations with native speakers. Taught in German. Prerequisite: German 232 or equivalent. Counts toward the German major. Open to non-majors. GE: FOL-G, ORC. Offered every Spring Semester.
