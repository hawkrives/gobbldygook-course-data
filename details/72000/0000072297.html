DANCE 321: Ballet III (0.25) (2010-2011, Semester 1)

This course offers a further exploration of ballet vocabulary and technique for the advanced dancer with an emphasis on clarity, intention, and musicality. Open to majors and other students with instructor's permission. Prerequisite: Dance 221. 321 may be repeated with instructor's permission. Offered each semester.
