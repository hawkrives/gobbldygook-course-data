ART 259: The Arts of China (2005-2006, Semester 1)

This course is intended as an introduction to the history of Chinese art, offering a survey of major artistic developments from neolithic times to the present. Among the topics to be considered: ritual bronzes; funerary remains of the Qin and Han; Buddhist sculpture; and the evolution of landscape painting. Important issues to be discussed include production and patronage, function, and borrowing and influence in the evolution of artistic works across time and space.
