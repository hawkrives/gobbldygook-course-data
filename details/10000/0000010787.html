NORW 111A: Beginning Norwegian I (2005-2006, Semester 1)

Proficiency in a second language opens the door to another culture and another way of viewing the world. This course starts students on the road to achieving such a proficiency. Students begin learning to speak, understand, read and write Norwegian and learn about Norwegian culture through the language.
