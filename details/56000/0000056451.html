WMNST 121A: Introduction to Women's Studies (2007-2008, Semester 1)

Required for the women's studies major and concentration, this course introduces students to the concept of gender as a category of analysis. It is designed for students who seek a fuller understanding of themselves as women and men and a wider knowledge of the experiences and achievements of women.
