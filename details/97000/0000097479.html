MUSEN 107: Viking Chorus (2014-2015, Semester 1)

The 85-voice first-year men's chorus performs a wide range of choral repertoire. Participation is by audition for first-year men only. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. May be repeated once for a maximum total of two semesters.
