CSCI 125: Computer Science for Scientists and Mathematicians (2014-2015, Semester 2)

This course focuses on handling data: visualization, finding patterns, and communicating with data. The primary tools are Python (for transforming data) and R (for visualization and classification). Students work individually and in teams to apply basic principles and explore real-world datasets with a sustainability theme. Prerequisite: calculus or consent of the instructor . Counts toward statistics and mathematical biology concentrations.
