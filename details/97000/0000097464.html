MUSEN 105: St. Olaf Chapel Choir (2014-2015, Semester 2)

This choir of 120 mixed voices performs a wide range of choral repertoire that includes larger works for choir and orchestra. Participation is by audition and does not include first-year students. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. May be repeated. Students in the St. Olaf Chapel Choir are encouraged to register for voice lessons.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
