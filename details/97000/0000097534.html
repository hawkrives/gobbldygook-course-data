ECON 220A: Economic Justice (2014-2015, Semester 2)

This course examines Christian ethics along with major frameworks in philosophical ethics (Utilitarianism, Liberalism, and Marxism) in addressing economic issues. Prerequisites: one of Economics 110-121 and completion of BTS-T or permission of instructor. Offered most years.
