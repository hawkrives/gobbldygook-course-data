MUSEN 111: Norseman Band (2014-2015, Semester 2)

This full symphonic band performs a broad range of repertoire for wind and percussion instruments. Participation is by audition. The ensemble conductor provides full details for all rehearsal and performance obligations. May be repeated. Students in the Norseman Band are encouraged to register for lessons on their band instrument.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
