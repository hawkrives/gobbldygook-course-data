MUSEN 112: St. Olaf Band (2014-2015, Semester 1)

The college's touring symphonic band of approximately 90-95 instrumentalists performs a broad range of repertoire for winds and percussion. Participation is by audition. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. May be repeated. Students in the St. Olaf Band are required to register for lessons on their band instrument.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
