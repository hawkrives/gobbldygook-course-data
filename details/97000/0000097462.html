MUSEN 103: Manitou Singers (2014-2015, Semester 2)

The 100-voice first-year women's chorus performs a wide range of choral repertoire. Participation is by audition for first-year women only. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. May be repeated once for a maximum total of two semesters.
