MUSEN 101: Collegiate Chorale (2014-2015, Semester 2)

This non-auditioned women's ensemble is open to students in all four class years as well as faculty, staff and emerita. Emphasis is on a less-demanding rehearsal schedule to create an enjoyable choral experience for all members. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. May be repeated.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
