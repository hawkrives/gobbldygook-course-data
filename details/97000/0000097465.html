MUSEN 106: St. Olaf Choir (2014-2015, Semester 2)

The college's touring choir of 75 mixed voices continues its original mission of primarily focusing on a wide range of sacred choral literature. Participation is by audition and does not include first-year students. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. May be repeated. Students in the St. Olaf Choir are required to register for voice lessons.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
