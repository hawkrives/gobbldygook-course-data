BIO 251: Plant Physiology (2014-2015, Semester 2)

This course begins with an in-depth look at a plant cell and its physiology, followed by a discussion of whole plant physiology as it relates to cellular functions. Students attend lectures CHEM 126. BIO 227 recommended. Counts as "comparative organismal biology" core category. Prerequisites: BIO 227, or BIO 125 and BIO 126, and CHEM 125 and CHEM 126, or CH/BI 125, CH/BI 126, and CH/BI 227 and BIO 126. Counts as "comparative organismal biology" core category.
