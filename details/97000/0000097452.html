MUSEN 121: St. Olaf Philharmonia (2014-2015, Semester 1)

This full symphony orchestra performs a broad range of repertoire. Participation is by audition. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. May be repeated. Students in Philharmonia are encouraged to register for lessons on their orchestral instrument.
