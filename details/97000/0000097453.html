MUSEN 122: St. Olaf Orchestra (2014-2015, Semester 1)

The college's touring symphony orchestra of approximately 90-95 instrumentalists performs a broad range of repertoire. Participation is by audition. The ensemble conductor provides full details of all rehearsal and performance obligations. May be repeated. Students in the St. Olaf Orchestra are required to register for lessons on their orchestral instrument.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
