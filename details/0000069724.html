MUSIC 343: Music Of The Classical And Romantic Eras (2009-2010, Semester 1)

Intensive study of musical literature between 1750 and 1900. The course begins with the development of the Viennese Classical School (Haydn, Mozart, Beethoven) and continues with European trends in Romantic opera, Lieder, symphony and chamber music (from Rossini through R. Strauss). Prerequisites: Music 241, 242. Offered Fall Semester 2009-10 and alternate years.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
