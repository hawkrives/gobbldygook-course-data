GCON 115E: The Tradition Continuing: The Romans and the Christians (2005-2006, Interim)

Students explore the Greek and Hebrew legacies in Roman society and in the New Testament, discussing various attempts to find personal fulfillment in political life, in stoicism and epicureanism and in the teachings of Christ and St. Paul. Students read works by Cicero, Horace, Virgil, Epictetus, the writers of the New Testament and study the artistry of Roman sculpture. Offered only in Interim.
