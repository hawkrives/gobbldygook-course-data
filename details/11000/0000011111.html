THEAT 150: Scenery and Costume Production (2005-2006, Semester 1)

This course focuses on the practices, techniques and materials used in the production of scenic and costume elements for stage performance. Students work on course projects in costume production, scenery production, visual research and computer-aided design. A course fee is required. Open to majors and non-majors. No prerequisite required. Offered once a year.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
