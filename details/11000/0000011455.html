MATH 210: Principles of Mathematics (2005-2006, Interim)

Selected topics demonstrate the scope and power of mathematics. Students will work on creative problem-solving, placing mathematical ideas within the context of their historical development and making connections to other disciplines. Intended for students with weak backgrounds in mathematics. Not open to first-year students. Prerequisite: Mathematics Placement Recommendation.
