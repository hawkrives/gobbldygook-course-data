AMST 301: Seminar (2005-2006, Semester 2)

This course undertakes an intensive study of a particular period or topic through the interdisciplinary perspective of American studies. This course employs a seminar format, with concentration on student research. Recent topics include: American Cultures, Mall of America, Disney's America,American Women of Color and Man and Nature. Offered Spring Semester.
