EDUC 379: Urban Education Seminar & Practicum (off-campus: Minn/St. P) (2005-2006, Interim)

Students working as tutors and teacher aides gain direct teaching experience in urban schools. This course is designed to heighten students' sensitivities to the complexities of the education of urban youth in a multicultural setting. Prerequisite: Education 290, 2.7 GPA, meeting all off-campus study requirements and satisfactory portfolio progress. The course fulfills Education 382 and partially fulfills clinical practicum requirements. Counts toward the major: American Racial and Multicultural Studies.
