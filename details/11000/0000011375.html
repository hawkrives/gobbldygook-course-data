DNCTH 124: World Dance Traditions (2005-2006, Interim)

An introduction to world dance traditions, this course examines dance forms, functions, contexts and differing aesthetics found in selected cultures of Africa, Asia, Europe, the Near and Middle East, the South Pacific and Latin America. The course includes lectures, discussions, video-viewing and required field-trips to dance events, in addition to studio experiences where students learn dances from the cultures studied. No previous dance experience required.
