FREN 272: Contemporary France (2005-2006, Semester 2)

Students are introduced to contemporary French political, economic and social institutions and/or issues through close textual analysis of articles from the contemporary French press and other media (e.g., the World Wide Web, cinema). Students read, analyze, discuss and write in French on a wide variety of non-literary topics selected by the instructor. Taught in French. Prerequisite: minimum of one 250-level course (two recommended).
