FREN 253: Introduction to Literary Analysis (2005-2006, Semester 2)

Students read a variety of French literary texts, selected by the instructor. Taught in French, the course focuses on aspects of literary analysis, terminology, methodology and literary history. Students develop critical skills through discussion and analytical writing. Prerequisite: French 232 or 233, or equivalent.
