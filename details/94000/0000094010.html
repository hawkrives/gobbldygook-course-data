MATH 120B: Calculus I (2014-2015, Semester 1)

This course introduces differential and integral calculus of functions of a single real variable, including trigonometric, exponential, and logarithmic functions. Derivatives, integrals, and differential equations are explored graphically, symbolically, and numerically. Applications of these topics are emphasized throughout the course. Prerequisite: mathematics placement recommendation. Offered each semester.
