PSYCH 244B: Psychology of Personality (2014-2015, Semester 1)

Students examine theories of normal personality development in an effort to understand the factors that shape personality. Is personality biologically determined? Is it a result of interpersonal experiences, learning and reinforcement? Are other factors involved as well? Students investigate prominent personality theories and research and their conceptualizations of this fundamental aspect of human experience. Prerequisite: PSYCH 125. Offered annually.
