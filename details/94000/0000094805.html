CSCI 252AL: Software Design and Implementation Lab (2014-2015, Semester 1)

Students investigate the implementation of software using strategies and concepts presented in CSCI 251, explore standard technologies for creation and management of multi-module software systems, and carry out stages of a life cycle-based team software project, through hands-on computational exercises and with direct support provided in a small group context. Prerequisite: concurrent registration in CSCI 251. Offered both semesters.
