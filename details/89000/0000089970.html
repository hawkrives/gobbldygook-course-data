DANCE 320: Ballet III (0.50) (2013-2014, Semester 2)

This course offers a further exploration of ballet vocabulary and technique for the advanced dancer with an emphasis on clarity, intention, and musicality. Open to majors and other students with instructor's permission. Prerequisite: Dance 221. 321 may be repeated with permission of instructor. Offered each semester.
