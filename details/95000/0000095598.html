PHYS 131B: Analytical Physics II (2014-2015, Semester 2)

Physics 131, the second course in the three-semester calculus-based sequence, treats electricity, magnetism, and electromagnetic waves. There is one 2.5 hour laboratory meeting per week. Prerequisites: PHYS 130 and concurrent registration in (or previous completion of) MATH 126 or MATH 128. Offered annually in the spring semester.
