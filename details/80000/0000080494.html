ART 225: Architectural Drawing and Design I (2011-2012, Semester 1)

Through a sequence of projects, architectural design students learn about drawing (hand drafting and computer-aided drawing and design) and architectural design processes that require the integration of social, artistic, technical, and environmental issues. Prerequisite: Art 102. Materials fee. Offered alternate years.
