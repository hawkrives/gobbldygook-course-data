MATH 396: Directed Undergraduate Research (2011-2012, Semester 1)

This course provides a comprehensive research opportunity, including an introduction to relevant background material, technical instruction, identification of a meaningful project, and data collection. Topics are determined by the instructor and may relate to his/her research interests. Prerequisite: determined by instructor. Offered in 2011-12 and alternate years.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
