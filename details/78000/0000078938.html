HIST 199: American History Since 1865 (2011-2012, Semester 2)

As they study the development of American institutions and society from the Civil War to the present, students examine economic, social, and political themes with a special emphasis on changing interpretations. Major topics are Reconstruction, urbanization, populism, progressivism, depression, New Deal, foreign relations, civil rights, social reform, equality for women, and other recent trends. Offered most years. Counts towards American studies and ARMS majors and ARMS concentration.
