DANCE 311: Modern III (0.25) (2011-2012, Semester 1)

This course builds on the concepts and components of Modern Dance I and II. Open to majors and other students with instructor's permission. Prerequisite: Dance 210/211 (at least twice) and/or permission of the instructor. Offered each semester. May be repeated eight times with permission of instructor.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
