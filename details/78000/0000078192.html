HIST 240: Major Seminar: Non-Western History (2011-2012, Semester 1)

This course explores topics in non-Western history, designed to emphasize active skills of critical reading, textual and contextual analysis, historiographical argument, and historical writing. The most recently offered topic: "American Empire: A Cultural History of US-Latin America Relations." Primarily for history majors; others by permission of instructor. Offered periodically.
