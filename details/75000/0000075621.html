ARMS 253: (2010-2011, Semester 2)

This lecture with discussion course explores famous African-American, Asian-American, Native American, and Hispanic artists like Faith Ringgold and Maya Lin who have amazed people with their art and have helped empower multicultural thinking in the U.S. through artistic, political and personal strategies. Students learn about art and ways these post-Civil Rights artists have addressed race, ethnicity and change in American society.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
