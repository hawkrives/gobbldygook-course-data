ENGL 284: Performing Arts in New York (off-campus) (2006-2007, Interim)

This course examines aspects of theater production in New York City. Students meet artists, directors, producers, critics, and other scholars skilled in the areas of performance and important to the critical reception of poetry and drama. They also tour relevant sites, developing skill at analyzing and evaluating artistic excellence, especially in the genre of review writing. Specifically, students consider three aspects of theater: written drama, play production and review writing. Offered in alternate years during Interim.
