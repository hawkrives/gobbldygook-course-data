ECON 111: Gateways to Economics (2006-2007, Interim)

These classes (110-120) introduce first-year students to principles of economics through special topics. Each course emphasizes the application of economic concepts to topics of general interest, capitalizing on the special interests and expertise of the faculty. A list of topics and course descriptions will be included with registration materials each semester. These courses are open only to first-year students.

To find books for this class, please visit the St. Olaf Bookstore.
