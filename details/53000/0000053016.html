MUSIC 264: Lyric Diction II (2006-2007, Semester 2)

Students study and practice the principles for signing in French and German with good pronunciation, enunciation and expression. Course content includes phonetic analysis using the International Phonetic Alphabet and identifying and exercising the phonemic features of French and German through class performances and listening assignments. Prerequisite: Music 263 and two semesters of voice lessons.
