MATH 352: Abstract Algebra II (2006-2007, Semester 2)

This course offers a continuation of group theory and field theory, including group actions, Sylow theory and Galois theory. Other topics may include representation theory, module theory and more, depending on the instructor. Prerequisite: Math 252. Offered in 2002-03 and then alternate years.
