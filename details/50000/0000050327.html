MUSIC 241B: History and Literature Of Music I (2006-2007, Semester 1)

Students encounter the history and development of Western European music from the Middle Ages to ca. 1750 and study the genre and styles of music from monophonic chant to concerted music of he Baroque. Prerequisite: Music 114. Offered Fall Semester.
