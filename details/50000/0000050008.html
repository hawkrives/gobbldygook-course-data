MGMT 229: Arts Management (2006-2007, Semester 1)

This course provides an overview of the key issues which face arts administrators. Topics addressed include planning, budgeting, fund raising, marketing and human resource management as each relates to the unique setting of the arts. This course will be offered as a semester class in 2003-04 and 2004-05.
