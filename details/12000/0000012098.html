MGMT 237: Managerial Accounting (2005-2006, Semester 2)

This course emphasizes the use of information to make management decisions. Topics include cost and break-even analysis, budgeting and control, capital expenditures analysis and variance analysis. Computer spreadsheets and case studies are used extensively. Prerequisite: Management Studies 225. Offered each semester.
