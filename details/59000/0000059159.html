GERM 232: Intermediate German II (2006-2007, Summer Session 2)

Students continue to explore life in German-speaking countries, supported by cultural readings, films, and other authentic materials that help students develop vocabulary and inspire them to advance their writing and speaking skills. Taught in German, the course includes a review of selected grammar topics. Prerequisite: German 231 or placement by test.
