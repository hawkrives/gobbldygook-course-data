ENGL 283: Studies in Prose (2006-2007, Summer Session 1)

Summer Session I, 2006 - In this course we'll read recently published novels from around the world that have compelling plots as well as those important philosophical, psychological, spitrual, and familial questions that face humans no matter where they live. Writers include Jonathan Lethem, Arundhati Roy, Barbara Kingsolver, Jhumpa Lahiri, Sue Miller, Nick Hornby, Zadie Smith, and Jonathan Safran Foer. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- FALL 2007 - Students explore various forms of prose, including fiction and creative nonfiction. Each instructor selects different material to be studied and emphasizes various aspects of the form. Topics can range from the 18th-century novel to African American autobiography. ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- May be repeated if topics are different.
