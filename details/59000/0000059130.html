MATH 128D: Honor Calculus II (2007-2008, Semester 1)

This course covers the material in Mathematics 126 in greater depth and includes supplementary material. Prerequisite: Mathematics120, 121, or Mathematics Placement Recommendation. Credit may be earned for either Mathematics 126 or 128, but not both. Offered both semesters.
