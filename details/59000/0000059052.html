EDUC 314: Social Studies in Elementary Classrooms (2006-2007, Summer Session 1)

This methods course introduces pre-service teachers to instruction, curricula, and assessment that characterize teaching for meaningful learning of social studies by elementary school children. Ancillary to the course are content, activities, and products of fine arts for elementary pupils, integrating the study of society with peoples' dance, art, music, and other forms of cultural identity.
