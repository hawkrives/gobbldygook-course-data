PHYS 386BL: 384, 385, 386 Advanced Physics Laboratory (2014-2015, Semester 1)

Experiments are selected from the areas of physics covered by PHYS 374, PHYS 375, and PHYS 376 respectively. Emphasis is on the development of good laboratory techniques and the ability to work independently. Each 0.25-course registration averages one afternoon of work each week. Offered annually in the fall semester.
