CLASS 253: Classical Studies in Italy (2015-2016, Interim)

This course introduces students to the history and art of ancient Italy, focusing on the city of Rome and the Bay of Naples area. It covers more than 1000 years of civiliation, beginningwith the Etruscans and ending with the fall of the Western Roman Empire. The itineraryincludesextended stays in Civitavecchia, Rome, Pompeii, Sorrento. When not visiting museumsandarchaeological sites, students have the opportunity to experience modern Italian culture aswell. Offered during Interim in alternate years. Counts toward ancient studies, classics, Greek andLatinmajors.
