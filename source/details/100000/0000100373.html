ART 227: Printmaking: Intaglio and Monoprints (2015-2016, Semester 1)

This course provides a strong foundation in the array of copper plate processes of etching,aquatint, lift ground, soft ground, and polymer plate printmaking processes as well as in painterlymonoprinting techniques. Students address personal sources of ideas, experimentation, drawingskills and compositional concerns and draw upon examples of master printmakers to discuss howimages reflect culture. Critical thinking skills are necessary in discussion and critiques.Materials fee. Offered annually. Prerequisite: ART 102.
