STAT 212A: Statistics for the Sciences (2015-2016, Semester 2)

A first course in statistical methods for scientists, this course addresses issues for proposing/designing an experiment, as well as exploratory and inferential techniques for analyzingand modeling scientific data. Topics include probability models, exploratory graphics,descriptivetechniques, statistical designs, hypothesis testing, confidence intervals, andsimple/multipleregression. Offered each semester. Enrollment limited for seniors. STAT 110, STAT212,and ECON 263 all provide an introduction to statistics and students should not take more thanone; they all can serve as a prerequisite for further courses. Counts toward the mathematicalbiology concentration.
